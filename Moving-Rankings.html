The Best Movies According to Natalie

My all-time top 3 movies and why!

The Shawshank Redemption

Honestly do I even need to explain this one...Morgan Freeman.
There is just something about his voice and his acting skills.
The story, the ending, I can watch this movie over and over again.

Pretty Woman

Julia Robert has always and will always be one of my favorite actresses.
Her smile and her laugh get me every time. She's a well rounded actor.
She makes you fall in love with her character in this movie.

National Lampoons Christmas Vacation

This one is for the nostalgia, it brings back so many great childhood
memories for me. We would watch this every Christmas
